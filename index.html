About us

What We Do

Aerospace Society, BIT Mesra is the official aero-modelling and drone research team of BIT Mesra. It is an interdisciplinary organization of about 45 undergraduate, postgraduate and PhD students with a mission to innovate, learn by applying theoretical concepts and create attractive opportunities for research and interaction with industry professionals. They design and fabricate aerial vehicles for various research and competitive applications. Aerospace Society was formed in the year of 2019 with the aim to raise awareness and igniting interest in the field of aviation and aerodynamics. It was formed with a vision to inculcate the ideas of aerospace into the students of undergraduate and postgraduate. The society aims to extract their innovative ideas and put it on to the right platform so that the strength of the students could be well recognized. It also aims to have a massive interaction of the students with the industry experts to understand the requirements of the industry. The students of this society get a prior exposure of the industry requirement which allows them to prepare themselves accordingly. It also helps to develop the students’ soft skills like teamwork, project management, communication skills and along with the technical skills to create a community of professional students, ready to take up new challenges and bring laurels to the institute and our country.
